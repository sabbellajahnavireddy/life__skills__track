#### 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?
- Unwelcome sexual comments, jokes, or remarks
- Inappropriate staring, gestures, or facial expressions
- Unwanted physical contact or invading personal space
- Sharing or displaying sexual or offensive content
- Sending inappropriate messages or emails

#### 2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?
- Ensure personal safety and the comfort of the affected person
- Note down details such as date, time, place, and people involved
- Report the incident to the ICC, HR, or as per PoSH policy
- Support the affected person and respect confidentiality
- Cooperate with the investigation process
